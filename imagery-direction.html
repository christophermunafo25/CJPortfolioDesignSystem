Photography rules

top-down · team at work
Hero · launch / pitch

Angle from above. Real desks, real artifacts. Always shoot at warm hour, never harsh noon. Lean orange-pink.

close-up · craft in progress
Storytelling · in-product moments

Hands, materials, screens. One subject, shallow depth. Stay below body-shot scale; we don't shoot brand-y portraits.

environmental · empty office
Atmospheric · section breaks

Spaces without people. Warm afternoon light. Let the mesh peek through windows or fabric.

product collage · papers + screen
Tactile collage · educational

Mix printed deliverables with the on-screen version. Lean into the "your team replicated" thesis.

Direction · do / don't

Real, warm, working. Show in-progress over polished delivery.
Stock smiles. No looking-at-camera, no thumbs-up, no hands-on-laptop poses.
Warm-side palette. Subjects sit in orange, peach, terracotta. Avoid cool blue floods.
Neon, holographic, futuristic. Not us. Hand-crafted beats sci-fi every time.
Diverse, specific, named. Real names, real teams, real outputs. Caption like a magazine.
Generic "marketers in a meeting". Specificity is the brand.
Grain on every photo. Apply the brand grain overlay; ties photo to the mesh.
Heavy filters or HDR. Stay natural; the grain is the filter.
