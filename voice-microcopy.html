Principles

Drop these in your head and most decisions write themselves.

Verb-first, plainspoken. "Generate", not "Submit". "Train voice", not "Save changes".
Specific over polite. "We can't reach Figma" beats "Something went wrong". Name the thing.
Sentence case everywhere. Buttons, labels, headlines. Title Case For Headlines Feels Corporate.
No exclamation marks. Not in product, not in errors, not in success. Confidence ≠ enthusiasm.
Cut filler. "Please", "Sorry", "Just", "Successfully" — almost always removable. Read it out loud.
Match what's on screen. Button label = primary action verb in the title. No surprises.
